About This App

This app was built with the intent of helping everyone that loans out their entertainment; Whether it be Games, Movies, Music, Software, we've got you covered. Simply put, you can manage everything from everyone. You can save all of your data from every loan you hand out.

We give you all the tools you will ever need. From Saving the Contact Info of who borrowed and what they borrowed. Collect their email address and phone number. Even leave some notes to help you remember anything else you need to know about the loaned entertainment.

So what are you waiting for? Start loaning out your entertainment and keep track of it. You cannot ask for a better peace of mind than this.

About the DateBox Plugin

This plug in solves an issue inside of the iOS simulator. By using it, it was able to assist me in correcting the date format that jQM uses to validate the input date.

Also, with this plug in, you get nice and nifty calendar dialog. Which is also a plus.
